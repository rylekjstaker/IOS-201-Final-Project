Why I Love Snowboarding

There's nothing quite like carving down a fresh powder run on a cold Utah morning. Snowboarding has been one of my favorite hobbies for years and Utah has some of the best mountains in the world right in my backyard.

What Makes a Great Snow Day

  1. Perfect Conditions
    • Fresh powder overnight
    • Sunny skies
    • Temperature around 25°F
  2. Great Company
    • Friends who keep up
    • Someone to hit jumps with
  3. Good Gear
    • Well-tuned board
    • Warm layers
    • Goggles with good visibility

My Favorite Utah Resorts
